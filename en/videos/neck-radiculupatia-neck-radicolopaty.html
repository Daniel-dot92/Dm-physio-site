What is shown in the video?

The video visualizes the nerve pathway or referral area. It helps show why symptoms can be felt away from the exact point where the irritation starts.

Why it matters

Nerve-like symptoms can have different causes. Understanding the pathway makes it easier to decide whether posture, muscle tension, joint position or medical assessment should be considered.

Next step

If the symptoms are strong, progressive, related to trauma, or include loss of strength or major numbness, seek medical assessment. For recurring pain or unclear symptoms, start with a functional assessment.
