Approach and practice

Ivan mainly works with musculoskeletal injuries, pain conditions and recovery after orthopedic and trauma-related problems. He has professional experience working with athletes and a particular interest in injuries and functional impairments of the knee and ankle.

His approach includes functional assessment, movement analysis and an individual plan based on the patient's condition, goals and everyday load.

He combines manual techniques, therapeutic exercise, dry needling, taping and cupping therapy. The goal is not only temporary pain reduction, but restoring function, improving movement control and gradually returning to sport and normal activity.

Training and focus

  • Manual techniques
