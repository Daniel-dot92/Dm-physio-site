Approach and practice

Elitsa works with people experiencing pain, injuries and movement difficulties, with a focus on musculoskeletal conditions and peripheral nerve symptoms.

She looks beyond the painful area at how a person moves, loads their body and compensates in daily life. Her approach is calm and structured, helping patients understand what may be happening and what they can do to support progress.
