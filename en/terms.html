Terms and Conditions

These terms describe how the DM Physio website should be used and how to interpret the information on it.

Educational information

The content on this website is for educational and orientation purposes. It does not replace a medical examination, diagnosis or emergency care.

Appointments

Appointment booking is handled through the external booking page linked from the site. Availability, changes and cancellations follow the booking system and direct communication with DM Physio.

Health caution

If you have severe, progressive or unexplained pain, trauma, loss of strength, major numbness, fever or symptoms that worry you, seek medical assessment.

Please do not misuse the website, forms, scripts or media. Text, images and videos are part of the DM Physio website and should not be copied or reused without permission.
